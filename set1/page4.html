The Soviet Union launched Sputnik 1, the first artificial satellite, marking the beginning of the space race.

Soviet cosmonaut Yuri Gagarin became the first human to orbit Earth aboard Vostok 1, a major milestone in space exploration.

NASA's Apollo 11 mission successfully landed humans on the Moon, with Neil Armstrong becoming the first person to walk on its surface.

The United States launched its first satellite, Explorer 1, marking its entry into the space race and leading to the discovery of the Van Allen radiation belts.

The Soviet Union's Luna 2 became the first spacecraft to reach the Moon, crashing onto its surface and proving direct lunar impact was possible.
